Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 7, 2024

Introduction

Tracking Device Detector ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Our app operates with minimal data collection:

  • Bluetooth Data: We scan for nearby Bluetooth devices to detect potential tracking devices. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Location Data: We request location permission as required by iOS for Bluetooth scanning. We do not collect, store, or transmit your location data.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, payment information is handled securely through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. We receive only basic subscription status information.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Scan for nearby Bluetooth devices
  • Display detected devices to you
  • Provide signal strength information for device location
  • Manage your subscription status

Data Storage and Security

All Bluetooth scanning data is processed and stored locally on your device only. We do not maintain servers or databases containing your personal information. Your data never leaves your device except for:

  • Anonymous analytics data to improve app performance
  • Subscription status validation through RevenueCat

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and payment processing
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing and app distribution

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored on your device
  • Delete the app and all associated data at any time
  • Opt-out of analytics by disabling them in the app settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
