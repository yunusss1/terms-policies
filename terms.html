2. Description of Service

Tracking Device Detector is a mobile application that scans for nearby Bluetooth devices to help identify potential tracking devices. The App provides:

  • Bluetooth device scanning
  • Device type identification
  • Signal strength monitoring
  • Suspicious device filtering (Premium feature)

3. Free and Premium Features

Premium Version:
